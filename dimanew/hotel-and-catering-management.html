HOTEL & CATERING MANAGEMENT (ICM)

This course provides the student with necessary skills in general hotel management.

It is ideal for people intending to develop a career as Hoteliers.

Course Duration 18months

Entry requirement KCSE D(Plain)

Units/Subjects Covered

  • Culinary Theory (Food Production Theory & Practical & Catering Theory)
  • Food & Beverage Management
  • Restaurant
  • Housekeeping & Accommodation Studies
  • Front office operation and Administration
  • Fundamental of Hotel & Catering
  • Customer Service
